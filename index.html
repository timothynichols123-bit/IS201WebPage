Let's talk about one of my favorite things

SKATEBOARDING

Resume Web App

Skateboarding has endless variety, it helps people develop strong character, and it helps create a sense of community. For this reason, I have created this page. I hope you enjoy as you get introduced to this sport that I have engaged with since age 12.

You can also look at some tableau.

Variety

  1. Street
    • Curbs; you can grind or slide on these

    • Rails; they are similar to curbs but more technical and scary

    • Flat; this includes any shoving or spinning, fliping, body rotation, or combination of these on flat ground

    • Stairs; they require speed to clear and you also gain speed as you descend. You can do any of the first three varieties over a set of stairs

  2. Park
    • Hips; this the corner of a bowl that bulges out. This makes it easy to catch air on

    • Snake Runs; these are several bowls merges together in line that snakes through the park

    • Half Pipes

    • SPEED! You can go very fast at a skatepark

  3. Vert
    • Big Fat Airs

Character

Skateboarding can help people learn determination and conquer fear.

Community

Skateboarding provides a physical outlet that can have a positive social impact, especially for youth. It can help kids stay out of trouble and help them believe they can do hard things. It surrounds them with people encouraging them to stretch and grow. Generally, it is positive community that encourages all skill levels to come and have fun.
